SEXUAL ASSAULT VICTIMS ASSISTANCEIN THE CITY OF URUGUAY

If you've been a victim of sexual violence, it is recommended to undergo a medical examination as soon as possible due to potential health risks, including sexual trasmition diseases. It is not required to file a complaint in order to receive medical and / or psychological treatment.

WHAT TO DO?

Go to the emergency of the Sanatorio Americano (address Puey 2533) or the nearest health center.

ttp://www.guiadigital.com.uy/Rubros/Salud/sanatoriosyhospitales.php
The doctor will make a record of that injury and take appropriate clinical action regarding medication intake for the prevention of pregnancy, HIV, STD and Hepatitis B, and psychological supportive care. If you want the Program Coordinator to join you you must contact 099-923-407 (mobile phone) / 27094891 (landaline)

FOR MAKING A COMPLAINT

You must go to the Seccional Policial de Violencia Doméstica which is opened the 24 hours. The address is San José 1126. From there it is derived to the court in order to set the measures to be taken in each case.
If you want the Program Coordinator to join you must contact 099-923-407 (mobile phone) / 27094891 (landline)

Early steps

-Register with the Consulate
-Make sure passport is valid for more than six months after the end of their stay in the site
-Always carry a passport copy with yourself and have a color copy at home, with the original
-Always have enough cash for a taxi
-Keep emergency elements handy for a carry-on luggage
-Gear List: a spare change of clothes, survival toiletries, water, medication, most important
documents, money, valuables and computer with charger

Immediate security procedures

-First, seek a shelter!!!
-Keep away from perceived and possible foci of danger
(dangerous streets, violent demonstrations, police repression, others)
-Contact the AD (Assistant Director) ASAP and inform of emergency location
-DO NOT MOVE till confirming that danger is off and transportation works
-Try to get home
-If isolated, find a safe place first, then contact the AD and inform
-Contact the AD (Assistant Director) AGAIN and inform of stable location
